About Ecofficient

The goal of Ecofficient is to help you lower your carbon footprint through our transportation application. Simply enter your starting location and your destination and let us do the rest! Our world class algorithm will compare weather conditions to your route selection and give you the best options for keeping your carbon footprint low.
